Code for Your Community

Join us for an exciting hackathon designed exclusively for students (grades 8-12). Whether you're a coding newbie or a programming prodigy, this is your chance to build something amazing, learn new skills, and compete for incredible prizes!

Event Schedule

Day 1 – Friday, September 5th

Goal: Kickoff, team formation, onboarding (GitHub), start hacking

5:00–6:00 PM Check-In Students arrive, register, get badges/swag. Background music + snacks.
6:00–6:30 PM Opening Ceremony Welcome speech, rules overview (safety + hack rules), sponsor shoutouts.
6:30–7:15 PM Team Formation & Icebreakers Facilitated networking games to help students find teammates.
7:15–7:45 PM Dinner
8:00–9:00 PM Workshop #1: GitHub 101 & Team Setup Hands-on workshop covering repos, branches, commits, pull requests & collaboration workflow.
9:00–10:00 PM Hacking Session #1 Teams brainstorm, wireframe, and begin coding. Mentors roam to help.
10:00 PM Dismissal Building closed.

Day 2 – Saturday, September 6th

Goal: Build → Polish → Present → Celebrate

7:00–7:30 AM Breakfast Students check back in; light morning stretch/energizer.
7:30 AM–12:00 PM Hacking Session #2 Teams work on MVPs. Mentors available for debugging and brainstorming.
10:00 AM–2:00 PM Mentoring Meeting with mentors
12:00–12:45 PM Lunch Break Catered meal TBD. Optional mentor office hours tables.
1:00–4:30 PM Hacking Session #3 (Polish & Demo Build) Focus on finishing features, polishing UI, preparing demos.
4:30–5:00 PM Final Hacking Prep Submissions due by 5:00. Teams organize for judging.
5:00–7:00 PM Judging & Demos Each team: 8 min pitch + 5 min Q&A with judges.
7:00–7:30 PM Dinner Break Quick meal while judges deliberate.
7:30–8:30 PM Awards Ceremony Announce winners (cash prizes, trophies, swag). Music, photos, networking.

Requirements

  • Must be in grades 8-12 (no college students)
  • Teams must have 2-6 members (no solo participants)
  • All projects must focus on environment, housing, public safety, downtown revitalization, or education
  • Can build apps, websites, or any digital solution
  • All skill levels welcome - from beginners to experts
  • Must register through our Discord/Slack community
