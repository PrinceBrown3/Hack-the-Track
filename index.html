Code for Your Community

Join us for an exciting hackathon designed exclusively for students (grades 8-12). Whether you're a coding newbie or a programming prodigy, this is your chance to build something amazing, learn new skills, and compete for incredible prizes!

Event Schedule

Day 1 – Friday, September 5th

Kickoff, team formation, onboarding (GitHub), start hacking

5:00 PM
Check-In

Students arrive, register, get badges/swag. Background music + snacks.

6:00 PM
Opening Ceremony

Welcome speech, rules overview (safety + hack rules), sponsor shoutouts.

6:30 PM
Team Formation & Icebreakers

Facilitated networking games to help students find teammates.

7:15 PM
Dinner

Fuel up for the night ahead!

8:00 PM
Workshop #1: GitHub 101 & Team Setup

Hands-on workshop covering repos, branches, commits, pull requests & collaboration workflow.

9:00 PM
Hacking Session #1

Teams brainstorm, wireframe, and begin coding. Mentors roam to help.

10:00 PM
Dismissal

Building closed. Get some rest!

Day 2 – Saturday, September 6th

Build → Polish → Present → Celebrate

7:00 AM
Breakfast

Students check back in; light morning stretch/energizer.

7:30 AM
Hacking Session #2

Teams work on MVPs. Mentors available for debugging and brainstorming.

10:00 AM
Mentoring

Meeting with mentors for guidance and feedback.

12:00 PM
Lunch Break

Catered meal TBD. Optional mentor office hours tables.

1:00 PM
Hacking Session #3 (Polish & Demo Build)

Focus on finishing features, polishing UI, preparing demos.

4:30 PM
Final Hacking Prep

Submissions due by 5:00. Teams organize for judging.

5:00 PM
Judging & Demos

Each team: 8 min pitch + 5 min Q&A with judges.

7:00 PM
Dinner Break

Quick meal while judges deliberate.

7:30 PM
Awards Ceremony

Announce winners (cash prizes, trophies, swag). Music, photos, networking.

Requirements

  • Must be in grades 8-12 (no college students)
  • Teams must have 2-6 members (no solo participants)
  • All projects must focus on environment, housing, public safety, downtown revitalization, or education
  • Can build apps, websites, or any digital solution
  • All skill levels welcome - from beginners to experts
  • Must register through our Discord/Slack community
