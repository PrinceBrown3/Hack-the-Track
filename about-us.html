Our Story

Hack the Track was founded by a group of passionate high school students who believe in the power of technology to transform communities. We saw a gap in Kentucky's educational landscape and decided to create the state's first high school hackathon.

Our mission is to provide students with hands-on coding experiences, mentorship opportunities, and a supportive community where innovation thrives. We believe that every student deserves access to quality technology education and real-world problem-solving experiences.

Through Hack the Track, we're building bridges between students, industry professionals, and educational institutions, creating lasting connections that will shape Kentucky's tech future.
